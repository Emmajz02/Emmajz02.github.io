To Love a Canine Rescue

"To Love a Canine Rescue, Inc. (TLC) is a non-profit, all breed, foster based, dog rescue based in Chester County, PA. Our mission is to find permanent homes for abandoned, stray and surrendered dogs in order to minimize the needless euthanizing of animals. Our dogs come to us from “put to sleep” lists at local and southern shelters, as well as from owner surrenders. We focus on making the right connections between our dogs and families in order to give the dogs the loving and permanent home they deserve. Our Mini Paws junior volunteer program is designed to train children, 4-18, in proper animal care and instill a love of community service, and a community outreach program to provide education on responsible pet ownership, including the importance of spaying/neutering, behavior training, and microchip implanting."

Beverly's Birthdays

"Since inception in February 2012, Beverly's Birthdays has distributed birthday gifts to over 36,000 children, created partnerships with 100+ social service agencies, expanded our work to support the "BIRTH"day with the launch of Beverly's Babies and The Greater Pittsburgh Infant Formula Bank. We offer our services based on our partners and families’ unique needs. All of our programs support children and families and help to let them know that THEY MATTER."

Pennsylvania Women Work

"Whether it’s a single mom struggling to provide for her family; a woman re-entering the workforce after 20 years; an immigrant adjusting to a new life; or someone faced with a challenging career transition; Pennsylvania Women Work is here to help lead individuals toward financial independence. By empowering women – and men – with job search and interview skills, opportunities to build their confidence, and help setting and achieving both professional and personal goals, we help individuals find lasting employment that pays a family-sustaining wage through unique and life-changing programs."
